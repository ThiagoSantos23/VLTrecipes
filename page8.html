CAIPIRINHA COFFEE

Ingredients

• Lime: Pick a fresh lime rather than a pre-bottled one as it might alter the cocktail flavor. Also, limes with soft, thin skin are the juiciest ones!

• Brown sugar: The caramel/toffee flavor pairs great with the nutty notes from the coffee as well as with the grassy taste of cachaça!

• Cachaça: It is an excellent Brazilian liquor made of the sugarcane juice.

• Coffee: Use good quality coffee to ensure a delicious cocktail. Also, you will want to make it strong because you will water it down with ice.

• Coffee beans: These are optional! Use them to garnish your drink.

How to make caipirinha coffee

1. Brew the coffee using the method you prefer. I used instant espresso powder, and it turned out delicious as it delivers a punch of coffee flavor!

2. While the coffee is brewing, muddle the lime and sugar in a cocktail shaker.

3. Add the cachaça, ice cubes, and espresso. Do not worry if the coffee is hot. After shaking it with ice, it will become cold in no time.

4. Now, shake it vigorously for approximately 10 to 15 seconds until the drink is cold.

5. Pour your caipirinha coffee into a highball glass filled with ice. Garnish with coffee beans.

6. Enjoy your drink!
