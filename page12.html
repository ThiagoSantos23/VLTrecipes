COCONUT COCKTAIL

Serving your coconut cocktail

• Like caipirinha, this coconut cocktail is made with cachaça, a Brazilian spirit.

• this Brazilian drink with coconut milk is easy to prepare.

• If you order this cocktail in Brazil, chances are you will drink it in a simple plastic cup with no garnishes. Unless you are in a fancy bar, of course.

• But to give it a nice tropical look, you can garnish your glass with a cocktail umbrella, mint, or coconut rim. All of these options make the drink the right vibe and look fantastic!

• For the coconut rim, dip the top of the glass into water or even coconut milk, then dip it into shredded coconut, rotating it gently to cover the edge evenly.

How to make a coconut drink

1. Follow these easy steps, and you will be sipping your delicious coconut milk cocktail in a couple of minutes!

2. Fill a pitcher with ice and set it aside. Most cocktails with coconut milk taste much better when cold, so this step is essential!

3. Next, put all ingredients in the blender and blend until creamy and frothy.

4. Pour the drink in the pitcher and serve! If you want, you can sprinkle shredded coconut on top of it.

5. Cheers!
