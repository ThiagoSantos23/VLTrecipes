SUNRISE COCKTAIL

Ingredients

• 2 ounces cachaça

• 6 ounces orange juice

• 1 ounce grenadine

• Ice cubes

• Optional: orange slice for garnishes

How to make a sunrise cocktail

1. Put some ice into a highball glass. Pour in the cachaça and orange juice, and stir them thoroughly.

2. Now, slowly pour the grenadine over the back of a spoon held over the top of the glass.

3. This guides the grenadine syrup down the side of the glass. It will sink to the bottom of the glass, creating a lovely red-pinkish layer at the bottom.

4. Do not stir the drink. Garnish with an orange wheel and cheers!
