RED WINE COCKTAIL

Tips to prepare the perfect cocktail with wine

• Use dry wine for this recipe as the sweetened condensed milk adds enough sugar to it.

• To have a nice and frosty glass, put it in the freezer for a couple of hours and remove it right before you pour the drink.

How to make this red wine cocktail

1. This wine cocktail recipe is very straightforward! Follow these easy steps, and you will be sipping it in a couple of minutes!

2. Fill a pitcher with ice cubes and set it aside.

3. Next, add the sliced pineapple or use the canned fruit. Remember to use unsweetened canned pineapple if you can.

4. In a blender, add the wine, sliced pineapple, and sweetened condensed milk, and blend it until smooth and frothy.

5. Finally, pour your red wine cocktail in the pitcher and serve immediately. Cheers!
