About VLT Recipes

VLT Recipes is here to make cooking food and preparing drinks easier and more enjoyable. We are delighted to present a collection of traditional Brazilian cuisine recipes.

Vanessa Carvalho, Leandro Damasceno, and Thiago Silva launched VLT Recipes in 2022 as a cooking app to share some of their favorite Brazilian recipes.

Select some of the food or drinks and enjoy!
